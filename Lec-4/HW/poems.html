The first rain
The figment of imagination hits the wildest when, My heart starts hankering after the courtship of a peacock and a peahen. Concomitantly savouring the palatable fragrance of the first rain, Washing away the inevitable hassle and pain. In a dense timberland away from cacophony, Into the lap of nature, with birds singing in euphony. The juncture when the dark cloud signifying dispiritedness, Brings smiles on the faces of peeps in blissfulness. The sinister lightning alluring the inner child, Wallowing into the nostalgia of the paper boats piled Gaping at the children reveling, Just embodying their lives while they are dancing. The beauty carries a charm of solicitude, Experiencing it with a cup of chai well brewed. In the depths of my heart I resemble a snail, Stepping out of the comfy habitat to witness the sky dropping pale. -Riya Hasija

Trust in pieces
Cerebration in wondering about disposition of each one, Even my own isn't quintessential but still all others are defyable. Some with diabolical perceptions pretending to be a bun, Pretentious, sweet , essential and on tip toe for u, how impeccable. Nothing could hurt more thn expectations, Facing slander hidden into deep incantations. What's the definition of mere confrere When each one ever trusted pretended to care. The idealogy of perpetual is a lie Destiny is always a goodbye Always thought for acquaintances before thyself Resulted in losing priority over oneself The indelible nostalgia of tears in agony Even after being divulged at times plenty I carried the trust defending it each time Lost it when words splurred out like a chime That twinged in and I lost myself in the moment Emotional quotient went into hinderment I jus hope to normalise things And forget knowing we are merely human beings. -Riya Hasija

Ainaa
Aaina tere ghar ka Gaya wo kahan? Dekhta tha jis me Tu khudko, Tera dil jawan. Dekhta Nahi Tu dil ko dekhta Nahi Is se hai roothaaa hua, hai ghar Gawa. Kaise ye raaaaste pe Tu Chal pada? Kaisi ummedein Tu lekar Khada? Hawaon me jo sukoon tha, ghar Tera unse hi yun ujad Gaya kya? Khwaboon me jo sukoon tha, wo hi khwaab Tera sukoon le Gaya kya? Aaina hua kyu gumshuda? Kahan Gaya tere ghar ka khuda? Kitabon me abhi tak fasa hua hai, kya? Kaali kahaniyoon ne tujhko, kassa hua hai, kya? Kaisi kahaniyoon me Uljha hua? Kahaaan Gaya wo ghar Tera suljha hua? Sitarooon me jo suroor tha, aasman Unseee hi Tera dhal Gaya kya? Sooraj ki Roshni se, ghar Tera is se hi yun jal Gaya kya? Aaina hua kyu gumshuda? Kahan Gaya tere ghar ka khuda? Toot Gaya, Khilonaaa Tera, Khwabooon ne tha, jo Jode rakha.Pyaaraa sa ghar, kyu Bihar Gaya? Darrdon ka nagma, kyu nikar Gaya? Aaina hua kyu gumshuda? Kahan Gaya tere ghar ka khuda? -Manya Parwani
